Course Title: Advanced Financial Accounting
Pre-requisities: BUS A-201 and BUS A-202
Course Location: New Zealand University
Credits: 3 How will this course advance student to the degree? This course is for students interested in learning more advanced financial accounting knowledge.
Anticipated frequency of offering: Once a year, Summer Semester
Access restriction: This course is only for students majoring Accounting or/and Finance
Course Details: This course is to prepare students for more advanced knowledge in financial accounting. More than half of the course is devoted to the related topics of investments in equity securities, business combinations, and the preparation of consolidated financial statements. The remainder of the course covers the translation of foreign currency transactions, the translation of foreign currency financial statements, and accounting for not-for-profit organizations.
Additional Comments: Textbook "Advanced Financial Accounting by James Smith and Linda Lee" is reqiured
